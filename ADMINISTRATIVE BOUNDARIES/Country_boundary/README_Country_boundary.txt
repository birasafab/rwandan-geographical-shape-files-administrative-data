Introduction

This country shapefile was created in 2001 by NISR  before starting the 2002 census mapping. This was digitized form the Rwanda Topographic map (1989)  prepared and published by IGN and Service National de Cartographie du Rwanda. 

Data source

This Country shapefile have been produced by NISR in 2001 from the 1989 Rwanda Topographic map

Attributes 

Name: Official Name of the country
Population: Total population (2012 Census)
